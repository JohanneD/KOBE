Robot design

I]Our vision

Our robot is called Kobe. We created Kobe with two goals in mind: Simplicity and efficiency. Our robot will therefore only rely two motors to navigate. He uses a gyroscope, a colour sensor and a touch sensor to know where he is in the arena. Finally to grab and throw the balls we fitted him with a pair of rotating arms.

II]Actuators

Kobe's main limbs are four motors. Navigation is insured by two motors. One motor is used for grabbing balls. The last motor's purose is to trow the ball.

III]Sensors

We spent a lot of time trying endow Kobe with the gift of sight (through a sonar) but didnt manage because of interferences with the opponent's sonars. Therefore it uses a gyroscope to find the angle and a colour sensor to know how far we are.
